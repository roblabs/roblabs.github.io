### Offline Satellite Map

We have hand-processed the NAIP image data for the Puget Sound area, stitched them together using a custom imaging pipeline with a whole ton of FOSS4G.

Our *Offline Satellite map for Mobile* is made to plugin to Avenza Maps <sup>1</sup>.   The file sizes of Offline Satellite maps for mobile can be large, so use the smaller samples to test out and become familiar with Avenza Maps.

[<img alt="Offline GeoPDF" src="/assets/img/avenza-matia.png" width="96" height="96" >](avenzamaps://s3-us-west-2.amazonaws.com/com.roblabs.yellow-dog/avenzamaps/tmp/ps/puget-sound-matia.tif)
